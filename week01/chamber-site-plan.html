Site Name

Brasil International Chamber of Commerce. Located in the state of São Paulo - Brazil, but covers the entire country

Domain name: https://www.iccbrasil.org/

Site Purpose

We are a non-partisan institution, which has a concrete engagement of members and works with international trade, as well as its entire orbit and ecosystem. We disseminate locally the content developed by the global ICC in its 12 areas of activity, organize events on themes of relevance to the country's economy, give a voice to companies installed in Brazil in the global arena, and transmit to the relevant government authorities the ICC's positions on key issues for international business. We are one of the only private sector organizations to have a global observer seat at the UN.
